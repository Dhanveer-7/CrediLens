# Real-World Kaggle Datasets for Loan Fine-Print Analysis

To train local classification models, fine-tune Gemini, or feed domain-specific context into the chatbot, you can leverage several verified, open-source datasets on Kaggle.

---

## 1. Legal Contract & Clause Extraction Datasets

### A. CUAD (Contract Understanding Atticus Dataset)
* **Kaggle Link**: [CUAD v1 Dataset on Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/konradb/cuad-v1)
* **Description**: A comprehensive corpus of legal contracts hand-annotated by expert attorneys to identify 41 critical clause types (e.g., liability limits, termination fees, governing law).
* **Best Use Case**: Fine-tuning models to identify hidden liabilities or high-risk legal clauses in loan agreements.

### B. Legal Indian Contract Clauses Dataset
* **Kaggle Link**: [Legal Indian Contract Clauses on Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rhtshrm/legal-indian-contract-clauses-dataset)
* **Description**: Contains contract clauses annotated with their type (e.g., Termination, Confidentiality, Fees) and risk level (Low, Medium, High).
* **Best Use Case**: Specifically calibrated for Indian financial legal structures to train classification models predicting risk severity.

### C. Synthetic Data Contracts (PDF Format)
* **Kaggle Link**: [Synthetic Data Contracts on Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/luizbueno/synthetic-data-contracts)
* **Description**: A collection of 30 synthetic contracts across various domains (real estate, vehicle, student loans) provided in PDF format.
* **Best Use Case**: Excellent for testing document loading pipelines, OCR accuracy, and text extraction parsers.

## 2. Financial Metrics & Loan Default Risk Datasets

### A. Loan Default Prediction Dataset
* **Kaggle Link**: [Loan Default Prediction on Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/nikhil1e9/loan-default-prediction-dataset)
* **Description**: Features borrower attributes such as age, income, credit score, and loan status (default vs. non-default).
* **Best Use Case**: Training local credit risk prediction models and comparing calculated loan parameters against borrower profiles.

### B. Credit Risk & Loan Default Analysis
* **Kaggle Link**: [Credit Risk and Loan Default Analysis on Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/urstrulyvikasv/credit-risk-and-loan-default-analysis)
* **Description**: Provides comprehensive structured financial and demographic information for credit risk assessment.
* **Best Use Case**: Training regression models to evaluate whether interest rates offered in agreements align with typical risk assessments.
